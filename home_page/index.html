About Plateful

  • Plateful is a recipe creation platform that helps you unlock delicious possibilities with the ingredients you have. Explore cuisines, create unique recipes, and enjoy cooking like never before.
  • Our features include a Meal Planner to organize your meals, a Recipe Generator to create recipes based on your ingredients, the ability to post your meals, explore others' posts, upvote your favorites, and comment to share your thoughts.

Meal Map

Plan your perfect week by adding, deleting, and dragging dishes into each day. Generate ingredient checklists to stay organized and enjoy a balanced diet with ease.

Plated Stories

Share your favorite recipes and connect with our lively community! Browse our recipe gallery to discover exciting new dishes, get inspired, leave comments, vote on your favorites, and save the ones you love.
